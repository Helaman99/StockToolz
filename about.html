Hi!

My name is Alan, and I'm the creator of this site!

I am a computer science major at Eastern Washington University, and I have a real passion for trading in the stock market. I do mostly swing and day trades, and have been since early 2021.

However, I noticed I was making a LOT of calculations manually every time I traded. So I decided to build some tools to do the redundant calculations for me so I could focus more on the markets.

But as an entrepreneur I also understand the potential value of creating websites and monetising them, which is why I decided to make my tools public and usable by anyone! So if you wouldn't mind spreading the word about these tools to all the savvy traders you know, I would be very grateful!

Happy trading!
